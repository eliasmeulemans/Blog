19/02/2016 // Stray From The Path @ Trix Antwerp

As if the energy in the room couldn’t get much higher from what’s already been established, I was pleasantly proven wrong with New York based hardcore quartet Stray From The Path. Though I wasn’t quite as familiar with SFTP’s material as the moshers around me, the energy emitting from frontman Drew York made it so both new and old fans could enjoy themselves and get lost in the music. Among one of the highlights of their performance, Stray From The Path went toward the roots of punk rock with a cover of The Ramones’ “Blitzkrieg Bop.” York made it a point for the audience to know their enemy, and ended their set with “Badge And A Bullet” off their 2013 release Anonymous, getting the crowd’s intensity at a high.
